Graphic Design 101: Your First Step Into Creativity

Graphic design is more than just colors and pictures—it's about solving problems visually. Whether you’re creating a logo or designing an app interface, graphic design plays a key role in communication.

It starts with basic principles like alignment, contrast, balance, and repetition. Understanding how fonts interact, how colors affect emotions, and how layouts guide viewers’ eyes is essential to becoming a great designer.

This blog sets the foundation for your creative journey, offering insight into the world of visual storytelling and digital design tools.
