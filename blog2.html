Why Typography Can Make or Break Your Design

Typography isn’t just about choosing a pretty font. It’s about readability, mood, and hierarchy. The right typeface can make your message stand out, while the wrong one can confuse or even repel viewers.

From serif vs. sans-serif to kerning and line spacing, understanding how to use text creatively can take your design from amateur to professional. Typography can express emotion, define brand identity, and guide the user’s eye.

Explore the impact of font combinations, text alignment, and dynamic scale in crafting clear and compelling designs.
