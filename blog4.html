Top 5 Free Tools Every Beginner Graphic Designer Should Use

You don’t need expensive software to start your design journey. There are many free tools that offer professional-level functionality for beginners. Here are our top 5 picks:

  • Figma: Powerful for UI/UX design and team collaboration.
  • Canva: Perfect for social media posts, presentations, and simple graphics.
  • Photopea: A free Photoshop alternative that runs in your browser.
  • Vectr: Simple tool for vector graphics and logos.
  • Krita: Excellent for digital painting and illustration.

Each tool has its strengths, and trying them out will help you discover your design style and workflow.
